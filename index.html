Frequently asked questions

Does Kanbanero work offline?

Yes. Kanbanero is a native macOS app that runs entirely on your machine. It never needs an internet connection, never phones home, and never talks to a server.

Where are my boards stored?

In a folder you pick. Every board is a plain .json file you can open in any text editor, back up, diff, or commit to Git.

Can I sync boards between Macs?

Yes — just put your boards folder in iCloud Drive, Dropbox, Syncthing, or a Git repo. Kanbanero stays out of it: sync is whatever you already use.

Can I use it with Git and pull requests?

Absolutely. Because boards are JSON, you can check them into your project repo, review changes in diffs, and keep task state alongside your code.

Is there a subscription?

No. Kanbanero is a one-time $8.99 purchase on the Mac App Store. No subscriptions, no accounts, no in-app purchases.

Does it work on Apple Silicon and Intel?

Yes — it's a universal binary and requires macOS 12 or later.

Is there an iOS, Windows, or Linux version?

Not today. Kanbanero is macOS-only. Since boards are plain JSON, you can read and edit them from any platform with any text editor.

Does Kanbanero track me or collect analytics?

No. No accounts, no telemetry, no tracking. Your data stays on your disk.
